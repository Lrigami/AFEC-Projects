The Best Lemon Pie Recipe

55 min

Very Easy

Cheap

Ingredients

For the shortcrust pastry:

  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 70g sugar
  • 125g butter
  • 250g flour
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 5cl water

For the lemoncurd:

  • 1 teaspoon of cornflour
  • 150g sugar
  • 4 lemons
  • 3 eggs

For the meringue:

  • 0.5 teaspoon baking powder
  • 100g icing sugar
  • 2 egg whites

Steps

Total time: 55 min

Preparation:

30min

Rest:

-

Cooking:

25min

  1. Begin with the shortcrust pastry. Preheat the oven to 180°C (gas mark 6). Separate the egg whites from the yolks.
  2. Whisk the egg yolks with the sugar and 2 spoonfuls of water until frothy.
  3. Mix the flour and the butter, cut into small cubes, with your fingers
  4. To obtain a sandy consistency and so that all the butter is absorbed (you need to work quickly so that the mixture doesn't soften too much).
  5. Pour the liquid mixture into the middle of this "sand".
  6. Quickly incorporate the ingredients with a knife without adding body.
  7. Form a ball with your palms and crush it 1 or 2 times to make the ball more homogeneous.
  8. Foncer un moule de 25 cm de diamètre avec la pâte sablée then cover with greaseproof paper and dried beans.
  9. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes (NB afterwards turn the oven down to around 120°C/150°C for the meringue).
  10. Continue with the lemon cream: wash the lemons and grate two to remove the zest.
  11. Place the very fine zest in a saucepan with the lemon juice, sugar and cornflour.
  12. Stir and start heating over a low heat.
  13. Beat the eggs in a separate bowl. Once the eggs are beaten, stir in the lemon juice, sugar, cornflour and zest.
  14. Place over a high heat and continue to stir with a whisk. The mixture will begin to thicken. Always stir when the eggs are added, as the lemon cream may burn.
  15. Remove from the heat and pour the mixture over the cooked tart base. Let rest
  16. Finish with the meringue: beat the egg whites until stiff with a pinch of salt.
  17. When they start to firm up, add the sugar and then the yeast. Whisk until the snow is firm.
  18. Cover the lemon cream with meringue then place the tart in the oven at 120°C/150°C until the meringue is golden brown (about 10 minutes).
  19. Enjoy !
